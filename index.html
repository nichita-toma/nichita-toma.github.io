General info:

GIS stands for Geographical Information System and is a type of technology used for integrating all types of data into maps to reveal certain things about a specific place. This computer system is part of a bigger study called Geographical Information Science, which is a at the root of the tools and technologies used in making sense of the physical world.

The power and relevance of GIS are increasing by day, as for example carbon sequestration models become crucial in mitigating climate change, or as mapping areas at risk of extreme climate phenomena becomes a necessity for survival.
